Official website for Track Titans, the student go-kart racing team of NIT Goa, competing at GKDC 2027 (Go-Kart Design Challenge).

🔗 Live site: track-titans-nitg.github.io/tracktitans


About

Track Titans is a first-time GKDC entrant from NIT Goa, built entirely by students — from chassis and steering to branding and this website. The site tracks our journey from design to race day: our progress through the competition phases, the people behind the build, and how sponsors and supporters can get involved.
